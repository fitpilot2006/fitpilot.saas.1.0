# FitPilot 1.0

Welcome to FitPilot 1.0 - Your personal fitness companion!

## Overview

FitPilot 1.0 is a comprehensive fitness application designed to help you achieve your health and wellness goals. Whether you're a beginner starting your fitness journey or an experienced athlete looking to optimize your training, FitPilot provides the tools and guidance you need.

## Features

- **Personalized Workout Plans** - Get customized exercise routines tailored to your fitness level and goals
- **Progress Tracking** - Monitor your improvements over time with detailed analytics and statistics
- **Nutrition Guidance** - Access meal plans and nutritional information to support your fitness goals
- **Community Support** - Connect with other users, share achievements, and stay motivated
- **Mobile-Friendly** - Use FitPilot on any device, anytime, anywhere
